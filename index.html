The pandemic is not over yet. We review each and every appointment to determine if you need to come in person or meet online with our dentists. An e-meeting with your dentist can save you time while making sure that you still receive the best possible care.

Meet our team

Connie Chiang Hedyeh Samady Anabelle Salonga Richard Kim Meghan McKenzie

After completing the UofM Dental Hygiene program Connie decided to further her education with the DMD degree. She enjoys spending time with her dog MuuMuu and trying new restaurants! Connie is happy to provide services in both Cantonese and Mandarin.

Dr. Hedyeh Samady was graduated in 2023 from University of Manitoba. Born, raised, and practiced as a hospital dentist in Iran. She moved to Canada in 2017. She enjoys taking the time necessary to ensure each patient has a full understanding of their oral health care needs.

Dr. Anabelle Salonga earned her Doctor of Medical Dentistry at the University of Manitoba. She is always seeking continuing education opportunities in varying dental fields ranging from cosmetic dentistry to mental health and cultural sensitivity courses.

On her spare time she loves to paint and uses her talents to make cards, and graphic designs for friends, family, and CHARM dental itself. Dr. Salonga is also fluent in both english and Tagalog.

Dr. Richard Kim graduated from University of Manitoba in 2023. He is passionate and dedicated to bringing a smile to his patients and enjoys working with patients. Richard also provides services in Korean.

Dr. McKenzie is a graduate of the University of Manitoba College of Dentistry. She enjoys pediatric dentistry, being apart of the CHARM family and caring for the surrounding community. Her soft spoken demeanour is sure to put you at ease. In her spare time she enjoys hiking, camping and watching hockey. Go Jets Go!

About Us

At CHARM Dental, we’re committed to providing you with the most exceptional care in a compassionate and friendly atmosphere. After many years of experience, we have earned the trust of numerous patients who suffer from dental anxieties and phobias.

Since the very beginning, our philosophy is that our patients come first and insurance issues come second. No matter the reason for your visit, we provide comprehensive and excellent care. Find out more about our providers and let us take care of you. Open since 2019, we have been continually learning and growing to provide YOU with the best services and experience!
